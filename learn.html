Grant Sanderson

The genius behind 3Blue1Brown, has an incredible knack for making math captivating. His intuitive explanations and stunning animations turned my math dread into love. With his engaging voice and imaginative approach, he brings math to life like no one else.

Josh Starmer

His use of clear, static visualizations helps break down intricate concepts into simple, digestible pieces.Starmer's approach builds understanding without oversimplifying, making the learning journey both insightful and enjoyable. BAM!!!

His "Very Normal" channel is a treasure trove for anyone looking to deepen their understanding of statistics. His intuitive approach to inferential hypothesis and his engaging teaching style have transformed my approach to statistics.

His depth of knowledge, clear explanations, and practical approach have made learning Python especially pandas, matplotlib, OOP, and Git much more engaging and effective.

Renowned for his practical, guided tutorials that make learning data analysis accessible and straightforward. His non-clickbaity approach ensures that the content is genuine and valuable, all offered for free.
